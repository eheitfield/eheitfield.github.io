Celestial Clock

See what's up in the sky above you!

With Celestial Clock you can quickly determine the position of the sun, moon, stars and planets as seen from any location on Earth.

  • Identify interesting sky features or plan your evening stargazing
  • Set up a widget to get a quick view of the sky from your home screen
  • Use multiple widgets to see the sky from different places on Earth
  • Turn any iOS device into a beautiful desk clock

Celestial Clock is designed to make learning about the sky above quick and easy. Tap the sky view to toggle between a natural display style that shows you just what's currently visible, or a star chart style that shows everything overhead, day or night. Rotate your device to switch between horizon and overhead views. Tap the settings icon to set your location; you can either use GPS to find your current location or drop a map pin to select somewhere else. Tap the clock to move forward or backward in time.

Celestial Clock is free to use and includes no tracking of any kind. Users may choose to voluntarily support continued development of this app througn an in-app purchase. Our privacy policy can be found here.
