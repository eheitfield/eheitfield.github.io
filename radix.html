Radix Clock

Some people ask, "who would want a clack that tells the time in binary?" I ask, "who WOULDN'T want such a clock?" Anyone can tell the time in base ten, but how many people know the time in base sixteen?

Key features of Radix Clock include:

  • show the time in binary, octal, decimal, or hexidecimal
  • digital or analog display
  • 12 or 24 hour clock
  • Keep Awake mode so you can use your device as a desk clock

Radix Clock requires iOS 13 and is designed to look great on any size iOS device.

Radix Clock includes no ads or web tracking of any kind. Our privacy policy can be found here.
