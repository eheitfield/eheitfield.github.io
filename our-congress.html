Our Congress

Your congressional representatives were elected to serve you, so make your voice heard.  Our Congress provides a simple way to follow the work of the United States House and Senate and contact your elected representatives. Our Congress brings together data from government entities and nonprofit organizations to put a wealth of information about Congress at your fingertips.

With just a few taps voters can use Our Congress to

  • contact members of Congress by phone, the web, or social media
  • review members' press releases, sponsored legislation, and voting records
  • identify members' largest campaign contributors
  • track bills as they work their way though the House and Senate,
  • follow the work of congressional committees,
  • review recent votes, nominations, and floor actions, and
  • bookmark representatives and bills for quick access.

Pterosaur Design has even included a copy of the U.S. Constitution for easy reference.

Our Congress was designed with accessibility in mind. Our Congress supports dynamic type on all screens so that the app is fully functional at even the largest font sizes.

Our Congress relies on data from a number of sources. ProPublica's Congress API provides up-to-date information on members of Congress and active legislation. This information is supplemented with additional data from The Center for Responsive Politics, the Library of Congress, and others.

Our Congress requires iOS 11 and supports both iPhones and iPads.

Our Congress is completely free and includes no ads or tracking of any kind. Our privacy policy can be found here.
