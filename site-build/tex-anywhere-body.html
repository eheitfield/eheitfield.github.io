TeX Anywhere

With TeX Anywhere you can quickly create and share beautifully formatted mathematical equations on your IOS device. Equations are input using standard LaTeX syntax and rendered in real time. Once complete, an equation can be exported to other apps such as Email, Pages, and Powerpoint as an image or PDF file. In addition, TeX Anywhere includes a stand-alone Messages app so you can send equations to your friends and colleagues from within Messages. If the receiver of your message also has TeX Anywhere installed they can edit your equation, making TeX Anywhere a valuable collaboration tool.

TeX anywhere includes a number of advanced features designed to make creating and sharing LaTeX-formatted equations quick and easy including <

  • a specialized keyboard toolbar providing quick access to commonly used LaTeX syntax characters,
  • dozens of pre-defined macros covering commonly used mathematical operators and symbols,
  • options to adjust an equation's font size, text color, and background (including transparency) to facilitating seamless integration of TeX Anywhere equations into existing documents,
  • support for many mathematical formatting commands and environments included in popular LaTeX packages such as amsmath, and
  • options to export equations as images, PDF files or LaTeX code.

TeX Anywhere requires iOS 12 and supports all sizes of iOS devices.
