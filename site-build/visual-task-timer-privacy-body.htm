Visual Task Timer Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to versions of Visual Task Timer available in the iOS App Store as of January 10, 2020.

At Pterosaur Design, your privacy is important to us.  We believe that the most effective way to protect your privacy is to collect and store only the bare minimum information needed to support the features you expect from our apps.  This document describes the data used by Visual Task Timer.

What data does Visual Task Timer store on your device?

Visual Task Timer stores a very small amount of non-personally identifying information on your devices in order to preserve the state of the app when it is removed from system memory.  This information includes user settings such as the desired color theme as well as information on the current state of the countdown timer.  If you back up your device, this information will be included in your backup files.

What information does Visual Task Timer transmit over the internet?

With the following exceptions, Visual Task Timer does not transmit user information over the Internet.

If you choose to provide feedback to Pterosaur Design via the app's "Send Feedback" button, you will have the opportunity to send an e-mail to Pterosaur Design which will be transmitted over the internet using standard secure protocols.

If you choose to support development of Visual Task Timer by making an in-app purchase (IAP), Visual Task Timer will communicate with Apple's servers to process this transaction. Please see Apple's privacy policy for information pertaining to Apple's management of this data.

Finally, depending on your iOS system settings, Apple may collect some information on the usage of all apps on your device including Visual Task Timer.  Please see Apple's privacy policy for information pertaining to Apple's collection and management of such data.

What information does Pterosaur Design store about you?

If you send us an e-mail, we will have your e-mail address and any other metadata associated with your message.  Pterosaur Design may use this information to reply to your message, as appropriate.

We will not use your e-mail address or other e-mail metadata for marketing purposes.

We will not sell your e-mail address or other e-mail metadata to any third party.

Questions?

If you have any questions about how we treat your data, please contact Pterosaur Design at pterosaurdesign@gmail.com.
