Radix Clock Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to versions of Radix Clock available in the iOS App Store as of June 5, 2020.

At Pterosaur Design, your privacy is important to us. We believe that the most effective way to protect your privacy is to collect and store only the bare minimum information needed to support the features you expect from our apps. This document describes the data used by Placemark+.

What data does Radix Clock store on your device?

Radix Clock stores a very small amount of settings information on your device. No personally identifying data is stored. If you back up your device, Radix Clock settings data will be included in your backup files.

What information does Radix Clock transmit over the internet?

If you choose to provide feedback to Pterosaur Design via the app's "E-Mail Pterosaur Design" button, you will have the opportunity to send an e-mail to Pterosaur Design which will be transmitted using standard secure protocols.

If you choose to support development of Radix Clock by making an in-app purchase (IAP), the app will communicate with Apple's servers to process this transaction. Please see Apple's privacy policy for information pertaining to Apple's management of this data.

Finally, depending on your iOS system settings, Apple may collect some information on the usage of all apps on your device including Placemark+. Please see Apple's privacy policy for information pertaining to Apple's collection and management of such data.

What information does Pterosaur Design store about you?

If you send us an e-mail, we will have your e-mail address and any other metadata associated with your message.  Pterosaur Design may use this information to reply to your message, as appropriate.

We will not use your e-mail address or other e-mail metadata for marketing purposes.

We will not sell your e-mail address or other e-mail metadata to any third party.

Questions?

If you have any questions about how we treat your data, please contact Pterosaur Design at pterosaurdesign@gmail.com.
